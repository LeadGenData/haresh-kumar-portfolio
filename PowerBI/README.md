# Business Intelligence: Triage Bottlenecks & Revenue Dashboards

This repository showcases how I turn raw clinical and sales logs into operational insights that executive leadership can immediately act upon.

## 🚨 The Problem: Operational Bottlenecks & Blind Decision-Making
Hospital ER managers and sales executives struggle to allocate resources effectively because critical operational metrics (like average patient wait times before seeing a doctor, or geographic supply chain margins) are trapped in flat CSV files and log databases. This results in understaffed departments, high patient frustration, and revenue leaks.

## 💡 The Solution: Interactive Visual Intelligence Dashboards
I built specialized dashboards to surface operational leaks and bottle-necks:
* **Hospital ER Analytics:** An interactive clinical dashboard that correlates patient intake volumes against triage severity and wait-time duration. Surfaced exactly which shifts and hours suffered from the highest delays.
* **DAX Semantic Layer:** Wrote custom DAX measures to calculate rolling average wait times, patient satisfaction margins, and shipment latency dynamically.
* **Sales Intelligence:** Designed map visualizers mapping profit margins against supplier shipping lanes to pinpoint unprofitable delivery regions.

## 📈 Business Impact
* **Clinical Allocation:** Enabled ER managers to forecast peak intake days, supporting a target 15% reduction in ER wait times through better staff scheduling.
* **Profit Recovery:** Highlighted low-margin distributors, allowing regional directors to renegotiate shipping contracts.
